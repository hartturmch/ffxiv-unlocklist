Aether Currents Guide

Aether Currents are expansion-zone progression objectives tied to flying. Each zone has a fixed set of currents, usually split between field pickups and quest rewards. Once you collect the full set for a zone, flying becomes available there.

Source: Aether Currents

How To Clear A Zone Efficiently

  • Do the MSQ in the zone first. Quest currents are often locked behind local quest progression.
  • Use the field current entries to clean up the map once the local quest line has opened enough movement routes.
  • Finish the remaining blue quests for quest currents after the MSQ branch is available.
  • Use the site filters to search by zone name and hide completed entries so each zone shrinks as you finish it.

What Matters Practically

Field currents

These are pure pickups. The important part is coordinates and route planning.

Quest currents

These depend on specific quests. The site preserves wiki links so you can open the original quest page directly when needed.

Zone grouping

The subcategory counter is the one to watch. If a zone says `7 / 9`, you know exactly how far that map is from flight.

Best usage pattern

Open one zone, finish it fully, collapse it, and move to the next. That keeps the checklist useful even as the dataset gets large.
