Weekly Checklist

Wondrous Tails

Use this when you already picked up, progressed, or turned in your current Wondrous Tails journal for the week.

  • Main NPC: Khloe Aliapoh in Idyllshire.
  • Best weekly use: pick up the journal after reset, clear easy unsynced duties, and turn it in once you have your preferred result.
  • Second Chance tools matter when you want to improve line outcomes rather than just finish 9 seals.
How To Do It
Jumbo Cactpot

Use this when you already bought your Jumbo Cactpot ticket or checked the weekly result and collected what you needed.

  • If the Gold Saucer quest is missing, you usually have not reached the level 15 MSQ gate yet.
  • Inside the Gold Saucer, speak with Jumbo Cactpot Broker at X:8.5, Y:5.9.
  • Hitting the Cactpot has no steps; talking to the NPC unlocks Jumbo Cactpot immediately.
How To Do It
Custom Deliveries

Use this when you already spent the weekly delivery allowances you wanted to use, or finished the client deliveries you planned for the week.

  • Custom Deliveries reset weekly and give scrips, gil, EXP, and client satisfaction progress.
  • You get 12 total allowances per week, with a cap of 6 deliveries per client.
  • Best routine: decide which client you are leveling or farming, then spend your allowances after weekly reset.
