Custom Deliveries Guide

Custom Deliveries are weekly turn-ins for crafters and gatherers. They give scrips, gil, EXP, and NPC satisfaction progress. The main weekly decision is where to spend your 12 allowances and which client you want to level or farm.

Source: Custom Deliveries

Weekly Rules

Allowance cap

You get 12 total delivery allowances per week.

Per-client cap

You can only turn in 6 deliveries per client each week.

Reset

Allowances reset weekly, so this belongs in your weekly routine instead of the one-time unlock list only.

Best use

Pick one or two clients you care about and spend your allowances there instead of spreading progress too thin.

System Unlock Path

Base collectables

Inscrutable Tastes unlocks Collectables.

Extra systems

No Longer a Collectable unlocks Aetherial Reduction.

Idyllshire suite

Go West, Craftsman unlocks the Idyllshire appraiser, scrip exchange, and vendor.

Later expansion suites

Rhalgr's Reach, Eulmore, Radz-at-Han, and Solution Nine each have their own unlock conversation quest.

What To Track Weekly

  • Spend the 12 allowances you care about after reset.
  • Check whether you are pushing satisfaction on a specific client or farming scrips on a capped one.
  • Keep one client focus if you want glamours, stories, or reward milestones faster.
