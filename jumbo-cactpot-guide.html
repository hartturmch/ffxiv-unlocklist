Jumbo Cactpot Guide

Jumbo Cactpot is the recurring Gold Saucer lottery. The one-time unlock is small, but it depends on the Gold Saucer being unlocked first, so it makes sense to keep it documented as weekly content rather than as a random detached unlock note.

Prerequisite: Unlock The Gold Saucer

Where to start

Go to Ul'dah - Steps of Nald.

NPC

Well-heeled Youth at X:9.6, Y:9.0.

Quest

It Could Happen to You

If it is missing

You usually have not reached the level 15 MSQ gate that opens this content yet.

How To Unlock Jumbo Cactpot

Where to go

The Gold Saucer, X:8.5, Y:5.9.

NPC

Jumbo Cactpot Broker

Quest

Hitting the Cactpot

Important detail

It has no actual steps. Talking to the NPC accepts and clears the unlock immediately.

What To Do Weekly

  • Go to the Gold Saucer and buy your weekly Jumbo Cactpot ticket.
  • Check the result on schedule and collect anything you need to collect.
  • Use the weekly checklist page as the reminder layer, not the permanent unlock page.
